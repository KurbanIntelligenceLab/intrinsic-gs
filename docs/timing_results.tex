\section{Reading Guide}
The SAM table reports classic TRASE-style SAM ViT-H automatic mask-generation time.  These are not SAM2 results.  The fair comparison reports the timed mask-production stages needed after checkpoints/data are available: for our method this is graph construction, graph symmetrization, clustering, and cluster-ID rendering; for TRASE this is SAM mask generation plus TRASE feature rendering.  Evaluation, visualization panels, method training, and one-time model/dataset loading are excluded from both sides where the artifacts expose stage timers rather than full command wall-clock.  The graph+cluster tables isolate our internal \texttt{spectral\_total\_s} component and should not be used alone as the headline runtime comparison.  Our timings were measured on NVIDIA A100-SXM4-40GB GPUs.
